1
Mental Disorder: A condition that affects your thinking, feelings, mood, and behavior
2
Depression: Shown through sadness, low self-esteem, loss of interest in activities
3
Schizophrenia: Causes hallucinations, illusions, and disorganized speech and behaviour.
4
Medication: used in order to treat patients based on what mental disorder the patient has
5
Psychotherapy: a therapy which teaches the patient how to deal with their problems and strategies to deal with certain situations.
